About him

Ernst Heinrich Philip August Haeckel was a German biologist, philosopher, physician, and artist. He is perhaps best known for his contributions to the fields of evolutionary biology, ecology, and embryology. Haeckel was a proponent of Darwin's theory of evolution by natural selection and helped to popularize it in Germany.

Haeckel is also known for his concept of "ontogeny recapitulates phylogeny," which posits that the development of an individual organism mirrors the evolutionary history of its species. He created detailed illustrations of marine life, which he used to support his ideas about evolution and to popularize science.
